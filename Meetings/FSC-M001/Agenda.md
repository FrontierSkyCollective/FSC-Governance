Purpose Statement
Frontier Sky Collective is currently in its formative stage.
The purpose of this meeting is not to finalize every aspect of the organization, but to establish foundational direction, guiding principles, initial priorities, and actionable next steps.
This meeting serves as the formal organizational starting point for Frontier Sky Collective.


Meeting Objectives
Upon adjournment, attendees should have:
Primary Outcome
Adopt Charter v1.0
Secondary Outcomes
Adopt Mission Statement
Adopt Vision Statement
Establish Organizational Identity
Approve creation of Governance Repository
Approve P001 in principle
Schedule future governance meetings
